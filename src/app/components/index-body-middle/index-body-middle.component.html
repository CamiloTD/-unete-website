Unete-io is a simple and powerful library that uses reflection for creating micro-services without messing with protocols.

Features

Your Micro-service is just an exposed JS Object! Callback support through the network! Connect to your Micro-service from the command line.
Instant method mapping in the client side Debug, start and monitor your service with unete-cli.
